Cromwell , I woll that yow send to me in hast the trussynn bed of cloth
of tyssewe , and the fether bed , wyth the fustyons and a materas
longynn to the same wyth the cownterpoynt ; GREY-1623-E1-P1,1.1,219.5

also I woll that you delyver all such tents , pavylyons , and hales as
you have of myne on to my sonne Lenard , as you tender my plesur .
GREY-1623-E1-P1,1.1,219.6

And thys shall be your suffycyant warrant and dyscharge att all tymes .
GREY-1623-E1-P1,1.1,219.7

Wrytyn at Bedwell thys present Thorysdaye by_foore our Lady daye the
Assumpcyon . GREY-1623-E1-P1,1.1,219.8

Cecyl Dorsett GREY-1623-E1-P1,1.1,219.9
