Right worshipful sister , after most harty and loving commendasions ,
with like desire of your good health and the long continuance of the
same ; this shall bee to give you most harty thanks for al your gentlnes
unto me shewid , and in especially for your goodnes shewid unto John
Pool my son : APOOLE-1552-E1-P2,258.5

he hath you most hartily commended , as your poor kinsman and bedesman ,
APOOLE-1552-E1-P2,258.6

and he desires you , and I both , to take no displeasur with him for his
long tariing here , APOOLE-1552-E1-P2,258.7

for as yet he is at no point for his childs part of my husbands goods .

Therfore , I desire you , as my trust is , that you will bee so good
Aunt unto him . APOOLE-1552-E1-P2,258.9

Also I desir to have me commended to my sister Clare and to my son

frends . APOOLE-1552-E1-P2,258.10

, and to Master Dennis , and to al other their good frends .
